Mediterranean Spiced Salmon

Tasty, spicy salmon that pairs well with a vegetable quinoa.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 20 ounces salmon fillets, (four 5oz pieces)
  • 8 lemon wedges
  • 1/4 cup parsley, chopped fresh

Directions

  1. In a small bowl combine salt, pepper, cumin, and paprika.
  2. Line a sheet pan or glass dish with foil and lightly grease with olive oil or nonstick cooking spray.
  3. Transfer salmon fillets to the pan and evenly coat the surface of each fillet with about 1/2 teaspon of the spice mixture.
  4. Place lemon wedges at the edges of the pan.
  5. Broil on high with the rack placed in the lower third of the oven for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked adn flakes apart easily with a fork.
  6. Sprinkle with parsley and serve with roasted lemon wedges.
