Chocolate Chip Cookies

Reminiscent of your grandma's cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 10 oz chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Mix wet ingredients, adding one egg at a time.
  3. Mix dry ingredients.
  4. Combine dry and wet ingredients.
  5. Add chocolate chips.
  6. On an ungreased sheet, spoon on cookie dough.
  7. Bake for 9-11 minute.
